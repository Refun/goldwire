Goldwire represents a global community sharing private investment opportunities.

Goldwire was initially started to help Chinese investors uncover opportunities along the Belt and Road. It is a work in progress and you will see improvements made continuously. You are welcome and encouraged to provide feedback directly onto the platform.

  • You will not see any names anywhere because the focus is on the quality of content and community. Given the global nature of the platform, we want to remove any biases around names or concerns about privacy / attribution.
  • We recently added the option for members to request connection to each other.
  • The single most important driver of this service’s utility is to build trust across the community and, when connecting, to treat every member with the utmost respect even if there isn’t a mutual fit. Please report any issues related to trust and respect to investor@goldwire.co. We will never compromise in this regard.
  • Also, please use clear, concise English when writing.
